Project

A Financial Analysis of the BSE Power Index

2024
This project examines the financial performance of the top five companies comprising the BSE Power Index — NTPC, Adani Green, Adani Power, Power Grid Corporation, and Tata Power. Using key financial metrics including ratio analysis, weighted average cost of capital, and working capital management, the study benchmarks each company's profitability, capital efficiency, and market risk. The analysis is further contextualized within India's ambitious green energy transition, positioning the power sector as one of the most critical and fast-evolving spaces in the Indian economy. Key findings include NTPC's dominance as index leader by market capitalisation (27%), Adani Power's leadership in return on capital employed (32%), and the sector's collective trajectory toward India's 2050 renewable energy mix target of 68%. The study maps each company's strategic positioning across the conventional-to-renewable spectrum, providing a holistic view of where value is being created and where risk is concentrated.
