Project

Investment Memos: A Collection of Startup Analyses

2025
Investment Memos features two high-growth startups — GlamPlus, a SaaS + B2B platform digitizing India's professional beauty industry, and Helloha, a Gen-Z focused social wellness app enabling meaningful, non-romantic connections through AI-driven interactions. Each memo follows a structured VC-readable format: market opportunity sizing, business model analysis, competitive positioning, key risks, and a clear investment thesis. The memos are designed to give a fund analyst-level view of each startup's potential. GlamPlus addresses a fragmented, largely offline industry worth billions, with a platform approach that aggregates salons, enables digital bookings, and layers SaaS tools for business owners. Helloha targets the loneliness epidemic among Gen-Z users with an AI-native product that facilitates genuine human connection without the pressure of dating dynamics.
