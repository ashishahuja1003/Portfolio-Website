Project

Green Groove Gourmet (G³)

2024
As part of a business simulation project, our team conceptualised and built G³ — Green Groove Gourmet — a cloud kitchen brand centred on regional Indian flavours and sustainable operations. The deliverable wasn't just a business plan; it was a fully functioning simulated startup, spanning brand identity, org design, HR documentation, financial statements, Gantt-based project management, and a live website — all built from scratch. G³ was designed around three core pillars: regional authenticity (celebrating India's diverse culinary traditions), sustainability (zero-waste kitchen practices, eco-friendly packaging), and scalability (a cloud kitchen model optimized for delivery-first economics). The project covered everything from stakeholder mapping and synergy analysis to financial projections and post-launch strategy.
